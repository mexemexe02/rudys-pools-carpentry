About Us

Rudy's Pools & Carpentry Shop

Based in Barrie, Ontario, Rudy's Pools & Carpentry Shop is a family-operated business built on a foundation of skilled craftsmanship and honest service. We specialize in transforming ordinary backyards into extraordinary outdoor living spaces.

Whether you're dreaming of a sparkling new pool, a spacious custom deck, or a complete backyard renovation, our team brings decades of combined experience to every project. We pride ourselves on clear communication, transparent pricing, and work that stands the test of time.
