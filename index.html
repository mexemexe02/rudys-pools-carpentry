Our Services

From pristine pool installations to custom carpentry masterpieces — we deliver end-to-end outdoor living solutions.

Pool Installation

Full in-ground and above-ground pool installation. Excavation, plumbing, electrical, and finishing — your backyard oasis starts here.

Pool Repair & Service

Leak detection, liner replacement, pump repairs, and structural fixes. We keep your pool running safely all season.

Custom Deck Building

Wood and composite decks — from poolside platforms to multi-level entertainer decks with built-in seating and lighting.

Custom Fencing

Privacy fences, decorative picket, and pool-safe enclosures built to Ontario code. Beautiful boundaries that add security.

Outdoor Kitchens

Complete outdoor cooking and dining areas with built-in grills, countertops, refrigeration, and weatherproof cabinetry.

Carpentry & Renovations

Custom built-ins, pergolas, gazebos, trim work, and structural framing. Precision craftsmanship for every project.
